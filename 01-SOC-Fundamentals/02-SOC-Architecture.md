# SOC Architecture

## What It Is

SOC Architecture is the structure of a Security Operations Center. It defines how people, security tools, and processes work together to detect and respond to cyber threats.

---
## Main Components

### People

- SOC Analysts (Tier 1, Tier 2, Tier 3)
- Incident Responders
- Threat Hunters
- SOC Manager

---
### Technology

- SIEM
- EDR
- Firewall
- IDS/IPS
- Threat Intelligence
- SOAR

---
### Processes

- Monitoring
- Detection
- Investigation
- Response
- Recovery
- Reporting
